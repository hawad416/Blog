About Gambia

The Gambia is a small strip of land located in West Africa. It is knows as the smiling coast of Africa due to the countrys location on the map.
It looks like a smile! It is Africas smallest nonisland country, but it is one of the most densely populated.It is a very beautiful country full of peace, happiness, and love!

Sights

1. Abuko Nature Reserve
The Gambias first reserve officially formed in the 1960's to protect a water collection point for nearby communities. Beautiful sight, and lots of fresh air!
2.Katchikally Crocodile Pool
Katchikally contains about 80 crocodiles. Locals believe that the water contains healing powers.
3. Tanji Fish Market
Come down to Tanji and see thousands of differnt types of fish, you can even take some of the tasty fish home to cook for dinner!
4. Bijilo Forest Park
Visit Bijilo Forest Park for a nice adventure with the monkeys in the forest. Bring nuts and bananas for them or else they might get cranky!
5. Brufut Beach
If you're looking for a nice place to relax and get away from the crowd, then Brufut Beach is the perfect spot for peaceful rest.
